# Vision: ClearMoney — The AI-Native Successor to Mint

## The Void
When Intuit shuttered Mint, it left millions of "financial power users" without a home. However, Mint was a product of a previous era: a **read-only ledger** that required manual effort to categorize and constant oversight to stay accurate. 

**ClearMoney is the agentic evolution of the personal finance manager.**

---

## Mint vs. ClearMoney: The Generational Leap

| Feature | Mint (Legacy) | ClearMoney (AI-Native) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Data Model** | Flat list of transactions | Unified Financial Context Graph |
| **User Input** | Manual tagging & budgeting | Natural language "Intent" setting |
| **Intelligence** | Rule-based "Budget Exceeded" alerts | LLM-driven "Decision Traces" & reasoning |
| **Integration** | Banking & Credit Cards only | Banks + Equity + Crypto + Real Estate + Debt |
| **Outcome** | A pretty chart (The "Dead End") | Autonomous Action (The "Action Layer") |

---

## The Three Pillars of the AI-Native Mint

### 1. Total Financial Context (The Graph)
Mint failed because it only saw half the picture. It missed the RSU grant at E*Trade, the $10k in a hardware wallet, and the real-time valuation of the home. ClearMoney aggregates every asset class—automated where possible, manual where necessary—to build a high-fidelity "digital twin" of your economic life.

### 2. Radical Transparency (The Decision Trace)
Old fintech used "black-box" algorithms to sell you credit cards. ClearMoney uses LLMs to provide **Explainable Advice**. Every recommendation comes with a "Why this?" trace that shows:
*   The raw data used (e.g., "Your $12k cash balance").
*   The mathematical rule (e.g., "Cash > 3mo expenses").
*   The reasoning logic (e.g., "Moving $5k to 4.5% APY yields $225/yr").

### 3. Agentic Execution (The Action Layer)
Mint told you that you spent too much on Starbucks. ClearMoney identifies that your student loan interest (6.8%) is higher than your HYSA yield (4.5%) and **drafts the transfer** to pay it down. You don't "manage" ClearMoney; you "authorize" the intents your agent prepares for you.

## Target Audience: The "Economic Citizen"
We are building for the user who:
1.  **Is Tiered of "Dead-End" Fintech:** They want their data to work for them, not just be displayed.
2.  **Has Complex Assets:** They have RSUs, crypto, and side hustles that Mint ignored.
3.  **Values Autonomy:** They want a "Self-Driving Car" for their money.
