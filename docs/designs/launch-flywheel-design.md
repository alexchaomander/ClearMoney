## Problem Statement
Financial anxiety is at an all-time high, yet trust in new platforms is low due to "Plaid Fatigue" and the failure of ad-supported models like Mint. People don't want a "Financial OS" initially; they want a specific answer to an immediate, burning financial question (RSU taxes, startup runway, etc.).

## Demand Evidence
- Users currently "stitch together multiple tabs and workflows" to get a fragmented picture of their finances.
- Historical precedent: Mint's massive adoption before its ad-supported model led to stagnation and shutdown.
- Market signals: High traffic to NerdWallet/TPG, but users are frustrated by "generic advice" not grounded in personal context.

## Status Quo
- Workaround: Manual spreadsheets and multiple browser tabs.
- Cost: Hours of "tons of time wasted," high anxiety, and missed financial opportunities (Tax Shields, RSU optimization).

## Target User & Narrowest Wedge
- **The Busy Founder**: Needs the **Founder Runway & Burn Tester** to know their survival time without bankrupting their startup or commingling funds.
- **The FAANG Tech Worker**: Needs the **RSU Tax Shock Analyzer** (AI Tax Shield Audit) before the April 15 tax deadline.
- **The Credit Card Maximizer**: Needs **The Honest Card Optimizer** (Shot #2) to cut through affiliate-driven valuations and see their real ROI.
- **The Wedge**: Single-purpose, "Show the Math" calculators that provide 80% of the value for free, converting to a waitlist for the full platform.

## Constraints
- **Date**: March 27, 2026 (Less than 3 weeks to Tax Day).
- **Security**: A committed encryption key (`STRATA_CREDENTIALS_ENCRYPTION_KEY`) exists in git history; must be rotated before tools go viral.

## Premises
1. **Context + Execution > Advice**: Knowing "what applies to me" and "how to do it" is the real problem.
2. **Transparency = Trust**: Users trust AI when they can see the deterministic "Decision Trace."
3. **Subscription > Free/Ads**: Users will pay for an advisor that is on their side, not an ad engine.
4. **Wedge Calculators = Viral Acquisition**: JIT tools overcome the high barrier to entry of a full OS.

## Approaches Considered
### Approach A: The Mini-Product Flywheel (Chosen)
Launch targeted "Shot" calculators (Founder Runway, AI Tax Shield Audit, Honest Card Optimizer) as standalone SEO-optimized landing pages to capture waitlist users and build trust.

### Approach B: The Trusted Full OS
Fix all P0 readiness items and open a gated public beta for the full platform immediately. Higher risk due to CAC and Plaid fatigue.

## Technical Definitions
- **AI Tax Shield Audit**: Utilizes AI extraction for public tax documents, then executes a constrained deterministic preview over the extracted signals. Broader tax-optimization coverage remains future work.
- **Decision Trace**: A human-readable logic tree generated by the deterministic financial core that explains exactly how a recommendation or calculation was derived (inputs, rules, assumptions).

## Privacy & Security
- **PII Minimization**: Calculators will only require the minimum data needed for the specific calculation.
- **Data Retention**: Unauthenticated user inputs for calculators are ephemeral and purged after 24 hours unless the user converts to the waitlist.
- **Key Rotation Plan**: 
  - Generate new `STRATA_CREDENTIALS_ENCRYPTION_KEY`.
  - Re-encrypt existing stored credentials (SnapTrade/Plaid tokens) in the production database.
  - Coordinate a 30-minute maintenance window for the git history scrub to avoid developer conflicts.
