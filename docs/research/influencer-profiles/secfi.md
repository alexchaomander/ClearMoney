## Key Insights from Secfi Research

### State of Stock Options Reports
Annual reports revealing industry data:

**2022 Report Findings:**
- 24%+ of startups reduced Fair Market Values
- Stock options = 86% of average startup employee's net worth
- Average client needs $846,000 to exercise options and pay taxes
- Equity still represents significant financial opportunity

### The Exercise Cost Problem
Many startup employees can't afford to exercise:
- Exercise price + AMT taxes can exceed savings
- 90-day post-termination exercise window creates urgency
- "Golden handcuffs" keep employees at companies they'd leave

### Equity Concentration Risk
Startup employees face extreme concentration:
- 86% of net worth in one illiquid asset
- No diversification
- Can't sell until liquidity event
- Job loss = income loss + potential equity loss
